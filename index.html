About Antaragni

Cultural Cell is established with the aim to develop the all- round capabilities of an individual, enabling students/ faculty to showcase their talents. The cultural cell aims to inculcate social and national values through development of various skills and performing arts among the student fraternity. The cultural cell gives a platform to showcase the arts and talents from different parts of the country. The cultural cell of DSATM also encourages and engage students to participate in cultural related activities across universities and various colleges in the region.
